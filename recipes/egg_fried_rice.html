Egg Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is Uncle Roger's fried rice recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp Peanut Oil
  • 5 smashed Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Shallot
  • 1 Egg and 1 Egg Yolk
  • 1 Spring Onion
  • 1 Red Chilli
  • 4 Cup Chicken Flavoured Rice
  • 2 tbsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Asian Sesame Oil
  • 1/2 tbsp MSG

Directions

  1. cook any chicken flavored rice that you want according to the directions on the package.
  2. While the rice is cooking, whisk the whole egg and the egg yolk in a small bowl, and set it aside.
  3. Next, coat a wok pan with peanut oil and put it over medium heat. Once it starts smoking, pour the excess oil out of the pan. Then add the garlic and shallot and fry them for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the beaten egg to the wok and stir it around until it's almost solid. Then add the cooked rice and mix it with everything in the wok.
  5. After it's mixed, add the soy sauce and sesame oil and flatten it into the rice with the back of a spoon.
  6. add the MSG, spring onion, and chili to the wok and cook for a minute or so, mixing all of the ingredients together.
